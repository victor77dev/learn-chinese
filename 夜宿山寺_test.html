《夜宿山寺》
李白 · Li Bai · Tang Dynasty
危楼高百尺，
手可摘星辰。
不敢高声语，
恐惊天上人。
The treacherous tower stands a hundred chi tall,
My hands could reach out and pluck the stars.
I dare not speak in a loud voice,
For fear of disturbing the celestial beings above.
